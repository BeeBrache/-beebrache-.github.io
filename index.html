About Me

Hello, I'm Bianca Brache. I am a dedicated and empathetic leader with a wealth of experience in technical and enterprise customer service, research, mentorship, and knowledge sharing. My heart lies in advocating for diversity, equity, inclusion, and empowering women.

In my personal life, I find joy in cooking, fine dining, and travelling. I cherish beach walks that end with invigorating cold plunges in the ocean. My greatest delight comes from spending time with my two sons, aged 4 and 13. Together, we explore creative outlets like digital graphic design, art, and literature.

Living with bipolar disorder has given me a deep understanding of mental health, and I am passionate about sharing my experiences to raise awareness and support others. This journey has strengthened my commitment to mental health advocacy and fostering inclusive environments for all.
